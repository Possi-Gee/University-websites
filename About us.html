About us

The best ICT university in Ghana

Ghana Communication Technology University (GCTU) is a leading public university in Ghana, specializing in the fields of communication, technology, and related disciplines. The institution aims to advance knowledge and practice in these areas through innovative education, research, and community engagement.
Founded: Originally established as the Ghana Telecom University College in 2005, the university was rebranded as Ghana Communication Technology University in 2018. This change reflected its expanded focus beyond just telecommunications to include a broader range of communication and technology disciplines.
Mission: GCTU's mission is to provide high-quality education and training in communication and technology, foster research and innovation, and contribute to the development of Ghana and the wider region through community engagement and partnerships.
Vision: The university aspires to be a leading center of excellence in communication and technology education, research, and innovation, recognized both locally and internationally for its impact on society and industry.
